## GTM Sequence

1. **Launch (months 1-3):** Personas 1, 2, 5 via PLG. Self-serve, acute pain, generate case studies.
2. **Growth (months 3-6):** Persona 3 as free-tier viral engine. Persona 6 discovers via SDR teammates.
3. **Scale (months 6-12):** Enterprise motion for Personas 4, 7. SSO, security reviews, sales-led.

## Persona 1: The Outbound SDR/BDR Lead

**Title:** Senior SDR, SDR Manager, Head of Outbound
**Company:** B2B SaaS, 50-500 employees

**Pain:** Personalizes outreach for 50-100 prospects/week but has no time to research each one deeply. When a prospect replies "send me something," they scramble for 45+ min in Google Slides.

**Current tools:** Apollo.io, Outreach.io, ZoomInfo (text enrichment only — never visual collateral)

**Switch trigger:** "Paste the URL, get a deck in 2 minutes" fits directly into outbound workflow.

**Budget:** $49-99/mo (within existing tool spend of $150-300/seat/mo)

**Online:** r/sales, RevGenius Slack (35k), LinkedIn "Sales Development" group (200k+), @Becc_Holland, @SamNelsonSDR, "30 Minutes to President's Club" podcast

---

## Persona 2: The Agency Founder

**Title:** Founder, Managing Director, Head of New Business
**Company:** Digital/marketing agency, 5-50 employees, $500K-$10M revenue

**Pain:** Each proposal takes 4-8 hours. 15 proposals/month = 255-375 hours. 22% average win rate.

**Current tools:** Canva, Google Slides, PandaDoc, ChatGPT for copy

**Switch trigger:** Auto-crawling prospect website. Cut 6 hours to 30 minutes. Agency-quality visual styles.

**Budget:** $99-249/mo (immediate ROI vs $75-150/hr senior time)

**Online:** r/digital_marketing, r/agency, DANG Slack, Jason Swenk YouTube, "Agency Profit Podcast"

---

## Persona 3: The Startup Founder Fundraising

**Title:** CEO/Co-founder (typically technical)
**Company:** Pre-seed to Series A, 1-15 employees

**Pain:** 2-4 weeks iterating on pitch deck. Zero design skills. Can't afford $3K-$15K design agency. Deck looks "AI-generated."

**Current tools:** Canva free, Slidebean ($29/mo), ChatGPT, Upwork freelancers

**Switch trigger:** "investor_pitch" archetype. Input VC firm URL → deck tailored to their thesis/portfolio.

**Budget:** High sensitivity. Free tier hooks them. $29-49/mo during active fundraise (3-6 month window).

**Online:** r/startups, r/SaaS, Hacker News, Product Hunt, YC Slack, IndieHackers, Slidebean teardown videos

---

## Persona 4: The Sales Enablement Manager

**Title:** Sales Enablement Manager, RevOps Manager
**Company:** Mid-market to enterprise, 200-5,000 employees

**Pain:** 65% of marketing content goes unused (Forrester). Reps waste 30% of time searching for content. Custom deck requests take 1-2 weeks.

**Current tools:** Highspot/Seismic/Showpad ($30-75/seat), marketing-created master decks

**Switch trigger:** Reps self-serve. Brand controls via mustInclude/mustAvoid guardrails. Analytics on which archetypes correlate with won deals.

**Budget:** Very low sensitivity. $20-50/seat × 50-200 reps = $12K-$120K/yr. Needs SSO + security review.

**Online:** Sales Enablement Collective Slack, RevOps Co-op, Pavilion, SE Summit conference

---

## Persona 5: The Solo Consultant / Fractional CRO

**Title:** Independent Sales Consultant, Fractional VP of Sales
**Company:** Solo or 1-3 person consultancy, 3-8 clients simultaneously

**Pain:** Sunday nights building 5-10 decks for the week. 40 unbillable hours/month on slides. Each client needs different branding.

**Current tools:** Google Slides + Canva per client folder, ChatGPT for copy, Loom as deck substitute

**Switch trigger:** Multi-business support (already in codebase). Input Client A's brand + prospect URL = deck in minutes. 10 decks/week × 4 hours = 40 hours reclaimed ($6,000-$12,000/mo at their rate).

**Budget:** $79-149/mo (immediate, obvious ROI)

**Online:** Pavilion ($500-$2,500/yr), RevGenius, r/sales, r/consulting, LinkedIn (their pipeline lives here)

---

## Persona 6: The Account Executive at a Scale-Up

**Title:** AE, Senior AE, Enterprise AE
**Company:** B2B SaaS, 100-1,000 employees, Series B-D

**Pain:** 15-30 active opps, each needing a "custom" deck (actually same 40-slide deck with 3 slides swapped). 1-2 hours pre-demo customizing instead of doing discovery.

**Current tools:** Internal Google Slides templates, Highspot/Seismic, manual Googling before meetings

**Switch trigger:** 5-minute pre-demo prep. Research layer surfaces insights they'd miss. Different archetypes per deal stage (cold → warm → competitive displacement).

**Budget:** $49-99/mo (trivial vs $500K+ quota)

**Online:** RevGenius, Bravado, LinkedIn social selling, @jaaborchuck, "30 Minutes to President's Club"

---

## Persona 7: The BD/Partnerships Lead at a Services Firm

**Title:** BD Director, VP Sales at IT services/consulting firm
**Company:** 50-500 employees, $50K-$500K deal sizes

**Pain:** Every deal needs a bespoke proposal. 3-5 people × 1-3 weeks per proposal. Loses to Big 4 with dedicated proposal teams. Junior staff spend 15-20 hours researching per deal.

**Current tools:** PowerPoint master templates, Loopio/RFPIO for RFPs, freelance designers

**Switch trigger:** Web crawl + AI replaces 15 hours of research per deal. Agency/case study archetypes. Consultative tone. $2,250 saved per proposal × 8-10/mo = $18K-$22K/mo savings.

**Budget:** Very low sensitivity. Would pay $249-499/mo team plan. ($500K+ annual BD budget)

**Online:** LinkedIn (primary), AMCF, Consulting Success podcast, Dreamforce/AWS re:Invent
